The Online Battleship Game

  1. Setup: Each player has a grid, usually 10x10 squares, where they can place their ships. The ships are placed vertically or horizontally and cannot overlap or go out of bounds of the grid.
  2. Types of Ships: Each player has a fleet of ships, typically consisting of the following:
    • Aircraft Carrier (5 squares)
    • Battleship (4 squares)
    • Cruiser (3 squares)
    • Submarine (3 squares)
    • Destroyer (2 squares)
  3. Gameplay: Players take turns shooting at coordinates to try to locate and sink the opponent's ships.
  4. Sinking Ships: If a player successfully hits a ship, the opponent marks the corresponding square as a "Hit." Once all squares of a ship are hit, the ship is considered sunk.
  5. End of Game: The game continues until one player has successfully sunk all of the opponent's ships. That player is declared the winner.
